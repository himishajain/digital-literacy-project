# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

##  DO:
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Keep your profile professional and respectful  
- Think carefully before posting anything online  
- Verify information before sharing it  
- Respect others’ opinions and maintain polite communication  
- Use privacy settings to control your data  
- Report suspicious or harmful content  

##  DON’T:
- Share personal information like OTP, passwords, or bank details  
- Post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content  
- Click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Spread fake news or unverified information  
- Share your live location publicly  
- Engage in online arguments or cyberbullying  
- Trust unknown friend requests blindly
